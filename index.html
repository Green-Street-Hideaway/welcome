Our Story

A curated local
experience, just for you

Green Street Hideaway was born from a passion for renovation and genuine hospitality. In late 2024, we transformed this ground-floor apartment into a warm, modern retreat — 2 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms, ideal for a family of four or a group of friends.

Nestled in the Lower Savinja Valley, you're surrounded by rolling hills, orchards, cycling paths, and crisp mountain air. Step outside to your private garden and terrace, fire up the BBQ, and let Slovenia's unhurried charm do the rest.

🌿 Did you know? "Zelena ulica" literally means Green Street in Slovenian — which is exactly where we live, and where the name was born.

Find us

In the heart of the
Savinja Valley

We're tucked away on a quiet green street in Šempeter v Savinjski Dolini — a charming town in the Lower Savinja Valley, surrounded by orchards, forests, and the gentle Savinja River.

Perfectly placed for day trips: ancient Roman history, thermal spas, riverside walks, and the famous Beer Fountain of Žalec are all within easy reach.

🍺
Beer Fountain Žalec — 4 km The world's first public beer fountain — free craft beer on tap!
🏛️
Roman Necropolis — walking distance Impressive ancient Roman burial site right in town
🚂
Celje Train Station — 14 km Historic city with a medieval castle; direct trains to Ljubljana
♨️
Rimske Toplice Thermal Spa — 31 km Relax in Roman-era thermal waters in a stunning valley setting
✈️
Ljubljana Airport (LJU) — 63 km About 50 minutes by car from the apartment
